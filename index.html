Our Story

Awbury Arboretum

The Francis Cope House, One Awbury Road, Philadelphia, PA 19138

---

Getting there

Awbury Arboretum is just under 10 miles from Center City Philadelphia and easily accessible by train or cab/Uber/Lyft.

---

By train from Center City Philadelphia

Awbury Arboretum is located right next to the Washington Lane station of the SEPTA Chestnut Hill East Regional Line. We suggest taking the 1:40pm train from Suburban Station, arriving at Washington Lane station at 2.04pm, leaving you plenty of time to enjoy the arboretum or for a pre-ceremony beverage. Tickets are $3.75 when bought at the station or $5 (cash only) when bought on the train. It's a 10 minute walk from the station to the Francis Cope House.

---

By cab/uber/lyft from Center City Philadelphia

Allow 25 minutes for the ride and expect to pay ~$20 (UberX/Lyft) or ~$40 cab. There will be plenty of guests to split rides with in both directions.

The (Un)Registry

We are so grateful we get to share our special day with you. We know some of you will be traveling far to be with us and your company is the best gift we could ask for. Please do not feel the need to give us anything else.

If this is unacceptable to you please understand that experiences and memories mean so much more to us at this point in our lives than any physical object could. Because of this, we have no registry - put your gift towards a trip to visit us, or for us to visit you, contribute to our honeymoon, suggest a restaurant you'd like us to go to, or an activity you'd like us to do... Please save the kitchenware and appliances for the day we own a house to store them in. Thank you!
