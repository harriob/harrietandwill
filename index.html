Harriet and Will's Big Day

Sunday May 28th, 2017


Ceremony and Reception

Our ceremony will begin at 3:30pm at Awbury Arboretum. Reception, lawn games and dancing will follow.

What to wear: Summer garden party attire. Shoes suitable for walking (and dancing in) on grass!

How to get there: Awbury Arboretum is just under 10 miles from Center City Philadelphia and easily accessible by cab/Uber/Lyft (recommended) or by train. More details about Awbury below.
