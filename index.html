Awbury Arboretum

The Francis Cope House, One Awbury Road, Philadelphia, PA 19138

---

Getting there (and back)

Awbury Arboretum is just under 10 miles from Center City Philadelphia and easily accessible by train or cab/Uber/Lyft.

---

By cab/uber/lyft from Center City Philadelphia

Allow 25 minutes for the ride and expect to pay ~$15 (UberX/Lyft) or ~$25 cab. There will be plenty of guests to split rides with in both directions.

---

By train from Center City Philadelphia

Awbury Arboretum is located right next to the Washington Lane station of the SEPTA Chestnut Hill East Regional Line. Tickets are $3.75 when bought at the station or $5 (cash only) when bought on the train. Trains run roughly once per hour. We'll provide a suggested train closer to the time.
